🛡️ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 June

At SafeSpace, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your experience is secure, respectful, and private.

🔒 What We Collect:

  • Account Info: Your name, email address, and login credentials when you register.
  • Mood Logs & Journals: Personal entries you choose to write and save.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve our services.

📁 How We Use It:

  • To personalize your experience.
  • To securely store your mood logs, journal entries, and drawings.
  • To improve website performance and features.

🤐 What We Never Do:

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not track you across other websites.
  • We do not read your journal or mood logs — they're encrypted and private to you.

👥 Your Rights:

  • You can request to delete your account and data at any time.
  • You can download your data or contact us for assistance.
